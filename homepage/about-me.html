About me

I am a software developer and chemistry student (UNaDM, BUAP) passionate about the intersection between computational science and research. My focus is on building robust technological tools to solve complex scientific problems. I am currently strengthening my algorithmic and computer science foundation through Harvard's “CS50's Introduction to Computer Science” course.

My experience is divided into two key areas:

  1. Scientific Development: I apply C++ to software development focused on the analysis of protein secondary structures.
  2. Web Development: I am specializing in backend development with Java, complemented by a solid foundation in frontend (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) validated by more than 300 hours of freeCodeCamp and Alura certifications.

I am actively seeking opportunities to merge my background in chemistry with software development to create innovative and accessible solutions for the scientific community.

Location: San Andrés Cholula, Puebla, Mexico.
